Manual Payment Guide
When to Use Manual Payments
  • Bank transfers from customers
  • Cash payments received
  • Check payments
  • Mobile money from other providers
  • Any non-M-Pesa payment method
Payment Process
  1. Select the vendor
  2. Enter payment amount
  3. Choose payment method
  4. Select plan to activate
  5. Add transaction reference
  6. Confirm payment
What Happens Next
  • Payment record is created
  • Vendor subscription is updated
  • Plan is activated for 30 days
  • Invoice is generated automatically
